About TSF

Teko Sports Foundation (TSF) is an Indigenous Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) incorporated with the Uganda Registration Services Bureau (URSB) on April 8, 2025 (Registration No: 80034915013327). Based in Gulu City, Northern Uganda, TSF operates in Omoro, Oyam, and Nwoya districts.

Vision: To empower communities and tackle critical challenges through innovative sports-based programs, fostering positive change in health, education, and social equity.

Mission: To create a Uganda where sports and games are catalysts for a healthy, equitable, and sustainable society.

Our Objectives

  • Health and Nutrition: Promote physical activity, healthy eating, and disease prevention through sports.
  • Education Outcomes: Use sports to improve school attendance, academic performance, and life skills, offering scholarships for talented athletes.
  • Community Empowerment: Foster peace, justice, and cultural preservation through inclusive sports programs, empowering marginalized groups.
  • Environmental Awareness: Educate communities about sustainable practices using sports as a platform.

5-Year Work Plan (2025–2030)

TSF aims to reach 15,000 children and youth annually by 2030, with indirect benefits to 50,000 people. Key activities include:

  • Year 1 (2025–2026): Pilot sports clinics in 10 schools (1,000 children) and host 3 community festivals (2,000 participants).
  • Year 2–3 (2026–2028): Scale to 30 schools (4,000 children), train 30 coaches, and promote peace through sports (7,000 participants).
  • Year 4–5 (2028–2030): Establish 3 community sports hubs, award 250 scholarships, and host a Northern Uganda Sports Summit (15,000 participants).
